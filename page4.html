Information of The Song

            
    
                
  • Artist: Taylor Swift
    • 
                
  • Album: 1989
    • 
                
  • Release Date: The song was released on October 27, 2014, with the album, and then as the second single on November 10, 2014.
    • 
                
  • Songwriters and Producers: Taylor Swift, Max Martin, and Shellback.
    • 
                
  • Genre: It is an electropop and synth-pop song.
    • 
                
  • Lyrical Theme: The song is a satire of the media's portrayal of Taylor Swift's dating life. It pokes fun at her public image as a "crazy, man-eating" woman who has a "long list of ex-lovers."
    • 
                
  • Music Video: The music video, directed by Joseph Kahn, features Swift as a wealthy, eccentric, and unstable woman who goes to great lengths to get revenge on her boyfriend after he breaks up with her. It was filmed at Oheka Castle and Woolworth Estate in Long Island, New York.
    • 
                
  • Chart Performance: "Blank Space" became a massive commercial success. It reached number one on the US Billboard Hot 100, replacing her previous single "Shake It Off" at the top spot. This made Swift the first woman to succeed herself at the top of the Hot 100.
    • 
                
  • Accolades: The song was nominated for three Grammy Awards, including Record of the Year and Song of the Year. The music video also won several awards, including Best Female Video and Best Pop Video at the 2015 MTV Video Music Awards.
    •
