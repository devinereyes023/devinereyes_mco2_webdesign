Exercise Reflection

In our exercise 8 we made a video demonstrating the use of float, overflow, height and width, background, and display position in HTML and CSS. Through this activity, I realized how these properties help control the layout and appearance of a webpage in simple but powerful ways. Float lets elements move to the left or right, overflow manages what happens when content doesnâ€™t fit inside a box, and height and width decide the size of elements. The background property helps set colors or images that make the page look more appealing, while display and position control how elements are arranged and placed on the screen. Learning these made me understand that even small code changes can affect the whole design, and practicing them helped me see how important organization and styling are in building clean and easy-to-read webpages.
